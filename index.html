Horizon

This is an online exchange platform for students, allowing them to get quick answers to their questions through a collaborative forum/ticketing system (instead of emails), to create content, or to share documents, articles, or events.
The idea comes from a recently established student support association at EFREI Paris. Its goal is to assist students in their academic success and to initiate collaborative projects for students
